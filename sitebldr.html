Welcome to Kadumane Hills Estates
The perfect destination for family holidays on a budget. Experience the outdoors and appreciate the surrounding natural beauty.

The Kadumane Hills Estate, surrounded by forests and lush green paddy fields heaven-like and out of the world. View of ranges of hills and sunrise and sunset set point are a delight to see, relax in the tranquility and give up any thoughts of the outside world. Added to it is the thrill of trekking, mountaineering or off-roading in the estate where you come close to nature.

Activities
Activities available to engage kids to senior people. Various difficulty levels treks can be arranged from our estate.
Accommodation
We have quaint Cottages for the couples and families, a Camping area with Tents and a Dormitory.
