Welcome to Kadumane Hills Estate

The perfect destination for family holidays on a budget. Experience the outdoors and appreciate the surrounding natural beauty. The Kadumane Hills Estate, surrounded by forests and lush green paddy fields heaven-like and out of the world. View of ranges of hills and sunrise and sunset set point are a delight to see, relax in the tranquility and give up any thoughts of the outside world. Added to it is the thrill of trekking, mountaineering or off-roading in the estate where you come close to nature.
